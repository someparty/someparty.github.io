Sponsorships

Some Party will have no more than a single sponsor each month. Sponsors are showcased below the site header with a logo and short write up, and are included in each newsletter sent during the month. As a courtesy to the readers, I'll only consider sponsors that fit the overall vibe of the project and I won't be running any banner ads, 3rd party tracking nonsense or anything else I find aesthetically questionable. Get in touch if you're a record label, venue, band, PR person, festival, brewery, or something in that vein.

The price is currently $25 CAD per month.
